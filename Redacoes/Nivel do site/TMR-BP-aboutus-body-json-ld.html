ABOUT US

Welcome to Mirror.co.uk - the award-winning website of Mirror Online, the Daily Mirror, Sunday Mirror and Sunday People.

Our team of experienced journalists will bring you the news you need to read, the videos you have to watch and the stories you want to share.

Download our app to keep the latest news at your fingertips and receive breaking news alerts on the stories that matter to you, and follow us on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram.

Our politics

The Mirror has consistently supported the Labour Party since 1945, but its first duty is always fighting for the best for its readers.

Read our leader column, Voice of the Mirror, for our views on the latest issues.

Our business

The Daily Mirror is published by MGN Ltd, part of Trinity Mirror plc, the UK's largest newspaper publisher.

Trinity Mirror publishes more than 150 newspaper titles and more than 80 websites. Find out which of our regional titles is nearest you, whether online or in print.

Registered office: MGN Limited, One Canada Square, Canary Wharf, London E14 5AP. Registered No. 2571173, England & Wales.

Trinity Mirror PLC is a member of and is regulated by IPSO, the Independent Press Standards Organisation. We adhere to the Editors’ Code of Practice.
